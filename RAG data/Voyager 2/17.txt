The Voyager Golden Record: Cover and Message of Hope

Each Voyager spacecraft carries a Golden Record, which is affixed to the side of the main bus. The record's cover features symbolic diagrams that detail how to play the record and decode the images, and include a pulsar map that shows the location of Earth.

The record contains a message of hope from U.S. President Jimmy Carter, which reads: "This is a present from a small, distant world, a token of our sounds, our science, our images, our music, our thoughts and our feelings. We are attempting to survive our time so we may live into yours." This message represents humanity's desire to reach out to the cosmos and share our existence with other civilizations.
