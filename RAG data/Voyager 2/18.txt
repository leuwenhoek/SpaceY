Identical Spacecraft Systems and RTGs

Voyagers 1 and 2 are identical spacecraft, each equipped with instruments to conduct 10 scientific experiments. These instruments include television cameras, infrared and ultraviolet sensors, magnetometers, plasma detectors, and cosmic-ray and charged-particle sensors.

Because the spacecraft travel too far from the Sun to utilize solar panels, they use Radioisotope Thermoelectric Generators (RTGs) for electrical power. These generators convert the heat produced by the decay of Plutonium-238 into electricity. This system has allowed the spacecraft to operate for over 48 years in the cold and dark environment of deep space.
