The Starbase Production and Launch Facility

The primary development, production, and launch facility for the Starship system is located in Boca Chica, Texas, and is known as Starbase. This facility includes a massive production site where steel rings are welded into rocket tanks, and a launch site located close to the Gulf of Mexico.

The Starbase launch site features an Orbital Launch Mount (OLM), which supports the Super Heavy booster and houses the propellant fill lines, and the Mechazilla catch tower. The pad is equipped with a high-flow water deluge system that sprays millions of gallons of water underneath the OLM during launch to suppress the acoustic energy and thermal forces generated by the 33 Raptor engines, preventing damage to the launch pad.
